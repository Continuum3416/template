The computer is unquestionably one of the most significant inventions of all time. It enables us to do almost anything in the modern world, such as weather modeling, but everything a computer does boils down to its ability to execute computation, which it does considerably faster than a human. The RK4 algorithm is a prime example of how powerful computers can be. If we set the number of iterations to one billion, it would take a person hundreds of years of non-stop calculations to finish, but today's computers can easily reduce that number to less than 10 seconds.
